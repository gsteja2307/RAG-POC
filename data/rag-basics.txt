RAG stands for Retrieval Augmented Generation.

RAG improves large language models by providing external knowledge during inference.

The pipeline includes:
- document ingestion
- chunking
- embeddings
- storage
- retrieval
- prompt augmentation
- generation

RAG is useful when models do not have access to private or recent data.

RAG systems reduce hallucinations by grounding responses in retrieved context.

Hybrid search combines keyword search and semantic search.
